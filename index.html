My Work

McMaster Baja Racing 2023 Controls System (Steering, Throttle and Brakes)

The Steering Rack consist of a 6061 aluminum body with a rack and pinion of module of 1.5 and a total travel distance of 80mm. This was found to be the ideal steering ratio for our driver to perform well in maneuverability The Brake System consists of 2 wilwood GS compact Remote Master cylinders and 3 wilwood gp200 calipers. This system gave us enough braking force to pass dynamic brake check. The pedal and bias bar focused on DFM using tube stock and waterjet parts to lower manufacturing and machine time. The Throttle System utilzing a push-pull cable attached to a rectangular tube(DFM). The driver is able to press the pedal to controls engine output

A-1 Cartridge Filler

The A-1 Cartridge Filler is a semi-automatic vape cartridge filler with the design goal of being affordable to it's target audience of small businesses. The design focus heavily on DFM/DFA elements. All in house manufactured parts had low cycle times of 20 - 30 minutes. Resulting in short lead times for customers. The A-1 Cartridge filler bridges the gap between manual and fully automatic cartridge filling giving small businesses an option to stay competitive affordably.

Helping Hand

During the 2021 MakeUofT Hackathon my team and I decided to create the “Helping Hand” a smart glove that provides exercises for a user to perform. By using a machine learning model the glove can recognize each successful try. We implemented flex sensors and an IMU on a glove to track the movement and position of the user's hand. An interactive GUI was created in Python to prompt users to perform certain hand exercises. A real time classifier is then run once the user begins the gesture to identify whether they were able to successfully recreate it. Through the application, we can track the progression of the user's hand mobility and appropriately recommend exercises to target the areas where they are lacking most.
